Record data from inside your car using your mobile device. The data that is recorded is similar to that of the black box recorder and flight data recorder in an airplane.

Using your mobile device, record video facing out the front windshield, record the audio in the car, and using the mobile device's GPS unit, track your current trip. Up to 10 mins before the stoppage of recording will be saved to your device and you can configure how long after you stop moving that the device stops recording. On detection of sudden deceleration, an email will be sent to your emergency contact with a map of your location and a button to dial emergency services will be available on screen.

FAQ

Which mobile devices are supported?
Devices with video recording capabilities and a built-in GPS unit. The ability to prompt the user to dial emergency services will be dependent on the device's operating system and the service available in the region.
How much does it cost?
The auto-message to your emergency contact is a premium service, billed at $0.99 per month.
